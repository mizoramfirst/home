Mizoram First

(Ideological Foundation)

Mizoram First chu thil dang zawng zawng aia Mizoram thatna tur dah pawimawh berna hi a ni. Mimal hlawkna tur emaw, party emaw, khua/veng hlawkna tur aia Mizoram pumpui leh Zofate thatna tur dah pawimawh berna, ram leh hnam hmangaihna dik tak hi a ni...
